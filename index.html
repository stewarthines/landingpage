Manage

In the oilfield, valuable time is spent managing jobs, and miscommunications can be costly. FieldSavvy streamlines those communications, allowing your workers on location to provide detailed, accurate instructions in a fraction of the time. Fields for job specifics including type of service, location and equipment required reduce the potential for miscommunication, and all communications from the start of a job through completion are automatically logged, creating a track record to refer back to whenever needed.

Select

Fieldsavvy makes hiring vendors simple.

At all stages of the job, your workers select oilfield service vendors. Whether you need waste material hauled, fluids transferred or rental equipment delivered, vendor hiring decisions come down to choosing the right company at the right price. FieldSavvy makes hiring vendors simple by standardizing the bid process, allowing managers to post bid requests and compare proposed rates while factoring in current market pricing as well as the vendor’s past performance.

Notify

Circumstances change quickly at a job site, and leaving vendors out of the loop can lead to unnecessary deliveries or pickups on the company’s dime. FieldSavvy users push real-time location and status updates to all vendors about changing conditions from equipment malfunctions to presence of harmful gas, so in case of a delay, vendors are notified and companies avoid paying for unnecessary onsite vendor visits.

Analyze

With printed work orders, telephone calls and paperwork, records quickly become outdated and difficult to find and sort. FieldSavvy connects users within a company to a shared database of vendor pricing and performance updated in real time as jobs are posted and completed at each work site. FieldSavvy’s filtering and data visualization tools put your current data to work and maximize your profit margins, and its precise track record of transactions promotes a culture of transparency and accountability throughout your organization.
